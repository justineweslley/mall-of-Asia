About SM MoA

Pasay City, Metro Manila

SM Mall of Asia is one of the biggest lifestyle destinations in the Philippines, located along the scenic Manila Bay. Once the largest mall in the country, it remains one of the biggest in the world, attracting millions of visitors every year.

The mall features four interconnected buildings with retail stores, restaurants, cafés, and attractions, alongside one of the largest parking facilities in the country accommodating around five thousand vehicles.

Visitors can enjoy the first IMAX Theatre in the Philippines, the Director's Club premium cinema, and the country's first Olympic-sized ice skating rink — also the largest in Southeast Asia.

Adjacent to the mall, the SM Mall of Asia Arena holds up to 20,000 people and has hosted major concerts, sports events, and the 2023 FIBA Basketball World Cup.

Attractions

World-class entertainment and landmarks inside and around MoA.

IMAX Theatre

The IMAX with Laser cinema features 4K laser projection, a 24-meter screen, and advanced surround sound. Seats 479 guests plus 9 exclusive VIP seats.

Olympic Ice Skating Rink

The SM Skating Rink spans 1,800 square meters on the third level of the Main Mall, accommodating up to 300 skaters at a time.

The MoA Globe

Made of steel and LED panels, the globe comes alive at night with animated displays at the main entrance facing Manila Bay — a must-see landmark and photo spot.

By The Bay

By the Bay is MoA's open-air leisure and dining strip along Manila Bay, known for sunset views, amusement rides, and nightly lights-and-music displays.

MoA Arena

The SM Mall of Asia Arena holds up to 20,000 guests and has hosted global artists, PBA, UAAP, NCAA, and the 2023 FIBA Basketball World Cup.
